## HOW TO CITE IN OUTREACH

**Always include 3 things:**
1. Company name (real, verified — never generic "a major bank")
2. Specific metric (never "significantly" or "dramatically")
3. Use case relevance (why it matters for THIS prospect's specific situation)

**Good example:**
"[Customer] reduced operational costs 30% and freed their team from weekend on-call rotations — your team is building on a similar architecture."

**Bad example:**
"Many companies have saved money with us." → No company named, no metric, no relevance.

---

## ⚠️ Before Using Any Proof Point

1. Verify the metric is still current — check the original source
2. Make sure the use case is actually analogous to your prospect's situation — don't stretch it
3. Never claim a customer uses your product unless it's confirmed in research
4. If the case study is old (2+ years), search for a more recent version
